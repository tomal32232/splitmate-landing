FAQ

Common questions.

Is SplitMate free?

Yes, completely free. No premium tiers, no ads, no hidden costs. All features are available to everyone.

Does everyone need to install the app?

Only the person adding expenses needs the app. Others can join via a link and view balances. But the full experience comes with the app installed.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. SplitMate is built offline-first. Add expenses, record payments, and view balances without any internet connection. Everything syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How does the AI receipt scanner work?

Take a photo of any receipt. Our AI reads the total amount, suggests a category, and fills in the description — saving you from manual data entry.

Can I use different currencies?

Yes. Each trip can have its own currency, and SplitMate handles exchange rate conversion automatically. Perfect for international travel.

Is my data safe?

Your data is stored securely with end-to-end encryption. We use industry-standard cloud infrastructure and never sell your data to third parties.
