Questions, answered.

When you add an expense you choose how to split it: equally, by percentage, by shares, or by exact amounts per person. SplitMate shows each person's share live as you edit, and you can include or exclude anyone from a given expense.

To share a trip ledger, yes — but joining takes seconds. Send an invite link, share a QR code, or give them the 6-character trip code. Tapping a SplitMate link drops them straight into the trip.

Yes. SplitMate is offline-first — you can add expenses, scan receipts, view itineraries and open documents with no connection. Everything syncs automatically the moment you're back online.

Personal documents stay on your device by default and are never uploaded unless you attach them to a shared trip. Sensitive types like passports and visas are locked behind Face ID, Touch ID or a PIN.

Three things: it reads receipts and bookings to fill in expenses and itinerary events, it suggests the right category as you type, and it answers plain-language questions about your spending in a chat — "how much did we spend on hotels?"

Absolutely. SplitMate supports 150+ currencies with live exchange rates. You can log an expense in the local currency and see balances in your home currency, with a clear note whenever a trip mixes currencies.

Yes — generate a full multi-page PDF trip report covering expenses, splits and settlements. It's saved in your Trip Reports library so you can re-download it any time without regenerating.
