FAQ

Common questions.

Is SplitMate free?

Yes — completely free. No premium tiers, no ads, no hidden costs. Every feature is available to everyone.

Does everyone need to install the app?

Only the person adding expenses needs the app. Others can join via a link to view balances. The full experience comes with the app installed.

Does it work without internet?

Absolutely. SplitMate is built offline-first. Add expenses, scan tickets, plan your day — everything syncs automatically when you reconnect.

How does the AI scanner work?

Snap a photo or upload a PDF of any travel document — receipts, boarding passes, hotel bookings, passports, visas. Our AI reads the text, recognises the doc type, and fills in the right fields. For tickets and bookings, it offers to add the event to your itinerary in one tap.

Can SplitMate replace my itinerary planner?

Yes. Add flights, hotels, and activities manually or scan tickets to fill them in automatically. Reminders fire at the correct local time at your destination — even if your phone hasn't switched timezones yet.

Are my travel documents private?

Your personal passport stays on the device you scanned it on — it never leaves unless you explicitly attach it to a trip. Other docs (flights, hotels) sync across your own devices and are visible to members of trips you've attached them to.

What happens if I sign in on a new device?

Everything syncs across devices: trips, expenses, itinerary events, and shared documents. The only exception is personal-vault passports — those stay on the original device.

Can I use different currencies?

Yes. Each trip can have its own currency with automatic exchange-rate conversion. Perfect for international travel and multi-currency expenses.

Is my data safe?

Your data is stored with row-level access controls — only you and members of trips you're part of can see your trip data. We never sell your data to third parties.
